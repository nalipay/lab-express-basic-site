Willy Wonka

My name is Willy Wonka and I am the owner of the Chocolate Factory. I am a tall, slender man, with brown hair who usually wears a plum colored overcoat that goes down to my knees over a black shirt with a W-shaped pin, a tall, black hat and golden chain (which seemed to be for a pocket watch). I was born from my mother on an unknown date, I was forbidden to eat any candy, because my father, Wilbur Wonka is a dentist who hates candy, especially chocolate. So, I left my home. But when I came back, my father was no longer there, and I was orphaned.
